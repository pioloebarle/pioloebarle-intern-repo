# Reflection

During my time as an intern at Focus Bear, I want to gain a deep understanding of how high-level software architectures are implemented and managed within a professional Agile environment.

I am most excited to sharpen my mobile development skills and broaden my knowledge about it. I have experience on mobile development through my school projects but I believe Focus Bear uses different tools which I am excited to learn more about it. I also look forward to improving my proficiency in asynchronous collaboration and mastering the Kanban workflow for efficient task management.

I have a strong foundation in terms machine learning and mobile development, as seen in my previous projects like PolyVision and Mama's Recipe, so I think my technical skills is sufficient enough to contribute to the team. Not only that, my technical curiosity and disciplined work ethic allow me to take ownership of mobile-specific tasks and delivera clean, well-documented code.

I feel less confident in managing large-scale cloud integrations and backend APIs, which is an area I am eager to strengthen during my 540-hour requirement. I hope to bridge the gap between my frontend mobile skills and backend data management to become a more well-rounded engineer.

Success means completing my 540 hours having made measurable contributions to the Focus Bear mobile app that improve the daily lives of its users. It also means evolving into a more confident professional who can navigate complex technical challenges with independence and a collaborative spirit.

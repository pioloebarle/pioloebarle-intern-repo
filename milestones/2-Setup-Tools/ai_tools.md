# Experiences using AI

The AI tools I tried are **ChatGPT**, **Gemini** and **Claude AI**. I used these tools to help me assist with coding and writing documentation. AI is exceptionally good at suggesting and sometimes fixing bugs in a block of codes. It also is good at explaining the code and how it works, if you are good at prompting to AI. However, it sometimes struggle to understand the context of the code and give me a solution that is not relevant to my problem. I sometimes to verify and understand the suggested code in order to not create any problems in the future.

AI is most useful during the initial research phase of a feature or when trying to locate a bug in a logic-heavy function. It is also a powerful tool for automating repetitive tasks, such as writing unit tests or formatting documentation, allowing me to focus on the more creative aspects of engineering.

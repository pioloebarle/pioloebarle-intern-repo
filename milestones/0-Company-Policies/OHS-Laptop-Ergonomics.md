# Reflection on Laptop Ergonomics

The equipment changes that I've made to improve my workspace setup is I started using a **laptop stand** to raise the screen to eye level. Aside from that, I also used external mouse for ergonomic purposes and also adjust the height of the chair so to avoid hunching over my workspace.

To mitigate physical strain, I applied the **20-20-20 Rule**. This method avoids eye strain when looking at my laptop for over long period of time. I'll also monitor my posture by conciously practice "sitting tall" to avoid back and neck strain. These small behavioral changes will help me on my physical health in my journey towards of being an intern.

To remind myself in caring my overall physical health, I will follow Focus Bear's schedule mandatory movement breaks. These ensure to stretch my body and move away from the desk at some time. I'll put also a small sticky notes on the corner of my workspace, just enough to see it and simply says "Sit Tall. Drink Water". This physical reminders help me to remind to care for my health during work.

**Reflection**

I will utilize AI primarily for drafting initial documentation interpreting complex error messages to speed up my workflow. As a Computer Engineering student, I have the engineering skills that are capable of analyzing and interpreting the core logics of my projects. AI will suggest a solution and what I do is I manually review and understand the solution so that not only it speeds up my workflow, but also understand the logic that the AI suggests.

If this results to delaying my workflow and spend at least 45 minutes, thats when AI come into play. I will seek AI for help by having an incremental steps rather than generating the whole code.

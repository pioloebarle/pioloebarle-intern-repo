**Reflection**

Agile ceremonies create a predictable rhythm that ensures every team member understands the current priorities and any technical "blockers" that might be slowing down progress. By having dedicated spaces for planning and reflection, the team can quickly pivot to solve problems without needing constant, unscheduled meetings.

As a Computer Engineering intern, I believe **Daily Stand-ups** are most important because they allow me to sync my technical progress with the team and get immediate help if I encounter a barrier or bugs. This ceremony ensures my work on Focus Bear features stays aligned with the overall sprint goals.
